Theme #1: Model Governance

The Challenge

Developmental gridlock due to poor multi-level governance, policy conflicts, and bureaucratic hurdles leading to inefficient fund allocation and wasted resources.

The Vision

Implement vital reforms to unify governance across Central, State, and local levels, optimize resource use, and accelerate progress through integrated data-sharing.

7 / 17

Theme #2: Clean Capital

The Challenge

Public health crises from poor waste management, lack of recycling, and the spread of diseases like dengue from stray animals.

The Vision

A centralized, AI-enabled system with predictive tracking and incentivized collection (like the Indore model) for a sustainable, healthy city.

8 / 17

Theme #3: Discover Ananthapuri

The Challenge

Limited promotion of cultural/heritage assets and untapped potential in medical, wellness, and backwater tourism.

The Vision

Unlock full tourism potential by integrating coastal communities and developing a futuristic coastal cruise model leveraging Vizhinjam Port.

9 / 17

Theme #4: Green Ananthapuri @2040

The Challenge

Urban mobility crisis with traffic congestion, poor connectivity, and high private vehicle usage due to limited green public transport.

The Vision

Achieve 95% public transport usage and Carbon Neutrality through EV subsidies, autonomous shuttles, and AI-driven traffic management.

10 / 17

Theme #5: Vibrant Trivandrum @2036

The Challenge

A significant skill gap in emerging sectors (AI, maritime), lack of startup spaces, and youth frustration hindering economic innovation.

The Vision

Boost youth engagement through civic leadership, create employment, and tap into youth energy with sports and organized eSports (like the Dubai model).

11 / 17

Theme #6: Reboot Thiruvananthapuram @2035

The Challenge

Lack of essential services in backward areas, including no street lighting, poor roads, and inadequate water, creating unsafe streets and poor quality of life.

The Vision

Focus on immediate liveability challenges by improving drainage to mitigate waterlogging, and provide public spaces and community facilities to foster well-being.

12 / 17

Theme #7: Tech Trivandrum @2047

The Challenge

A weak digital infrastructure, absence of large-scale AI/startup incubators, and limited collaboration between academia and industry.

The Vision

Achieve global leadership by developing an ethical AI Commons and launching the Thiruvananthapuram Research and Innovation Cluster (T-RIC).
